Food and Dining

Food and Dining at Wonderla Park, Hyderabad

Explore a variety of flavours at Wonderla Hyderabad where we treat your tummy to shades of happiness. Our Hyderabad amusement park offers four multi-cuisine eateries to choose mouth-watering meals from. There are also snack counters and coffee shops for your dining pleasure. You can get your favourite vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes at our eateries.

Multi-cuisine restaurants at Wonderla Hyderabad

Galaxy AC Restaurant

Offering a rich multi-cuisine vegetarian and non-vegetarian buffet, the dishes at Galaxy are nothing short of heavenly.
Food Court
Enjoy a variety of delicacies ranging from pizzas to pasta, refreshments to Oriental flavours, and local favourites, topped off with some delicious sundaes from Swirls.
Waves Restaurant
Get the best of multi-cuisine specialties at Waves. Enjoy combo meals with kebabs and biryani along with other detectable Chinese combos.
Park View Restaurant
Start the day with a traditional spread of Indian breakfast.
